The GOATx DeFi exchange was built from the ground up for cryptocurrency token traders on the Binance Smart Chain. GOATx was created by developers experienced in stock and commodities trading with beginner, intermediate, and professional traders in mind. Unlike previous DeFi exchanges like Uniswap and Pancakeswap, GOATx provides token collectors and traders with accounting, tracking and analytic tools traders expect. GOATx uses the MasterChef and liquidity pool smart contracts from Pancakeswap to provide over $3.4B in token liquidity while giving users tools they expect from trading platforms like RobinHood, eTrade, or TD Ameritrade.

Unlike centralized cryptocurrency exchanges like Binance and Coinbase that are operated by a single, corporate entity, decentralized exchanges (DEXs) allow for trading without an intermediary. Due to the expensive transaction fees on Ethereum, GOATx is focused on BEP-20 tokens found on the Binance Smart Chain.

What is GOATx

GOATx is a decentralized exchange that allows you to trade cryptocurrencies and tokens without a centralized intermediary, keeping custody of your tokens all the while. It is built on automated smart contracts deployed on Binance Smart Chain, the blockchain platform run by crypto exchange Binance. GOATx provides an easy to use interface with trading tools that would be expected by stock traders but are lacking in most DeFi exchanges.

Like many other DEXs, GOATx is built on an automated market maker (AMM) system, which relies on user-fueled liquidity pools to enable crypto trades. Rather than dealing with an order book and finding someone else who wants to swap the tokens you have for the ones you want, users lock their tokens into a liquidity pool via smart contracts. That allows you to make the swap you want, and users who keep their coins in the pool earn rewards all the while.

How does GOATx work?

Token swaps take place via liquidity pools between token pairs. Users are able to exchange one type of token for another without an intermediary, while other users who stake their tokens in the liquidity pools earn a share of the rewards generated by transactions.

What is special about GOATx?

GOATx is built with a focus on user experience. The biggest complaint about DeFi exchanges is that they are not user friendly and they do not provide tools to help the user track their portfolio. When you begin using GOATx, you immediately have access to portfolio management features for every trade you have ever made on the Binance Smart Chain including BNB and all BEP-20 tokens.

Getting Started with GOATx

You will need your own crypto wallet to use GOATx, as the DEX does not support fiat currency. GOATx works with wallets such as Trust Wallet and MetaMask. Although MetaMask was originally only an Ethereum wallet, it can be easily configured to work with Binance Smart Chain. Unlike centralized exchanges, you won’t have to input a bunch of personal data—you don’t even have to create a profile.
