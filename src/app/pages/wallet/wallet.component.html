Connect your Wallet

Requirements

All browser applications connect to the blockchain using a browser extention that controls your wallet and keys. This ensures your transactions are safe and only you can access your tokens or NFTs. Your private keys are stored inside your wallet and never sent over the internet.

Installing Metamask

The most popular wallet is Metamask.

You can download and install Metamask here: https://metamask.io

Part 1: Installing Metamask

Step 1: Download Metamask

You can download and install Metamask here: https://metamask.io


Step 2: Click "Add to Chrome"

Click “Add to Chrome” to Install MetaMask as Google Chrome extension.


Step 3: Click "Add Extension"

Click “Add Extension” to confirm and MetaMask will be added You can see that MetaMask is added by the little fox logo that shows up on the top right corner.


Part 2: Creating a Wallet

Step 1: Click on the Metamask Logo

Step 2: Read and agree to the Metamask Terms and Conditions

Step 3: Click “Create a new vault”, or “Create new wallet”. This depends on your browser.

Step 4: Enter a strong password and click “OK”

Step 5: Metamask will now show you your seed. It is very important that you copy and store those 12 worlds, without them you cannot restore your wallet.
